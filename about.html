About Us
Welcome to Pine City Mall, your premier shopping destination since 2023. Located in the heart of Cape Town, we offer a diverse range of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment experiences.
Our Story Founded with the vision of creating a vibrant community hub, Pine City Mall has grown into a cornerstone of Cape Town. Over the years, we’ve expanded to include Amazing restuarants aswell as our High end luxury stores, ensuring an ever-evolving experience for our visitors.
What We Offer Discover a mix of high-end boutiques and everyday essentials, alongside a variety of restaurants and entertainment options. Enjoy our modern amenities, including ample parking, free Wi-Fi, and accessible facilities, designed for your comfort and convenience.
Community & Sustainability We’re proud to host regular events, from fashion shows to charity drives, fostering a sense of community.Making us a responsible part of Cape Town.
