About Us
Louis Vuitton Malletier, commonly known as Louis Vuitton is a French luxury fashion house and company founded in 1854 by Louis Vuitton.The label's LV monogram appears on most of its products, ranging from luxury bags and leather goods to shoes,perfumes, watches, jewellery ETC. Louis Vuitton is one of the world's leading international fashion houses.
